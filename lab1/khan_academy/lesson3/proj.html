Recipe 1:Steak

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Beef steaks 2 pieces
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Garlic 2 cloves
Fresh rosemary 2 sprigs
Butter 2 tablespoons
Salt and pepper to taste

Steps for preparing:

Step 1: Heat a pan over medium heat and add the olive oil.
Step 2: Place the steaks in the pan and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness.
Step 3: Add crushed garlic and rosemary to the pan, then add butter. Baste the steak with melted butter for extra flavor.
Step 4: Serve with your favorite sides.

Recipe 2: Caesar salad with chicken

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breasts 2 pieces
Romaine lettuce 4 cups
Croutons 1 cup
Parmesan cheese 1/4 cup
Caesar dressing 1/4 cup
Lemon 1 piece
Black pepper to taste

Steps for preparing:

Step 1: Cook the chicken breasts (grill or bake).
Step 2: Slice the chicken into strips.
Step 3: In a bowl, combine the romaine lettuce, chicken, croutons, and Parmesan.
Step 4: Drizzle with Caesar dressing and toss gently.
Step 5: Sprinkle with freshly ground black pepper and serve with lemon wedges.

Source: Caesar's recipe website
